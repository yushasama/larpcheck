Kazuma's Eris Heist
Statement

In his past life, Satou Kazuma was a NEET who spent approximately 4,000 hours playing Steal-a-Brainrot on Roblox. He died embarrassingly. Aqua laughed at him.

Now isekai'd into Axel, Kazuma has realized that the kingdom of Belzerg's road network is just a weighted tree and that his past-life brainrot has made him the most dangerous thief in the land. The Eris Church is not prepared.

The kingdom has n towns connected by roads forming a tree. Each town i has ri Eris coins sitting completely unguarded because Darkness used her connections to throw a festival far off and everyone is celebrating. Aqua's contribution is 0.

Kazuma picks one town as his hideout and dispatches the party to steal from every town simultaneously. However, the further a town is from the hideout, the longer it takes Megumin to waddle there (she refuses to run, it messes up her hat), so more guards have time to respawn.

The haul from town v when hiding at town u is rv × (n − d(u, v)) Eris, where d(u, v) is the number of roads between them. Your total haul from hideout u is the sum of rv × (n − d(u, v)) across all towns v.

For each town u from 1 to n, output the total haul. Kazuma wants the most optimal hideout. Aqua will cry either way.

Input

The first line contains a single integer t (1 ≤ t ≤ 104), the number of test cases.

Each test case begins with a single integer n. The next line contains n integers r1, r2, ..., rn (1 ≤ ri ≤ 109), the Eris stored at each town. The next n-1 lines each contain two integers u and v (1 ≤ u, v ≤ n, u ≠ v), a road between two towns.

Output

For each test case, print n space-separated integers on a single line, the total haul for each hideout from 1 to n.

Constraints
1 ≤ t ≤ 104
1 ≤ n ≤ 2×105
1 ≤ ri ≤ 109
Towns are numbered 1 to n (1-indexed). Roads are undirected.
1 ≤ u, v ≤ n for each road, u ≠ v. No self-loops.
Sum of n across all test cases ≤ 2×105
Notes
Hideout 3 gives the best haul of 54 Eris. Kazuma correctly identified it as the most central node relative to coin density. Aqua called it beginner's luck. Megumin exploded town 5 anyway for the aesthetic. Aqua will cry either way.

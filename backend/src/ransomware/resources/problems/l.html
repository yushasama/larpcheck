Kazuma's Course Launch Party (The Urus Is Already Leased)
Statement

The Isekai Trading Matrix just went live on Whop. First week sales covered exactly the Urus lease payment and nothing else. Kazuma declared this a win and immediately spent the remaining budget on a course launch party. Aqua wanted an open bar. Megumin wanted a pyrotechnics display. Darkness wanted to get rejected at the door. Kazuma overruled all of them and spent everything on influencer appearance fees.

There are n influencers in the fintwit scene. Each influencer i charges an appearance fee of ci coins. Influencers are connected through a network of m mutual collabs and shoutouts. The total reach of a group is the number of unique influencers in the combined follower network reachable from any member of the group through the collab graph (including the invited guests themselves).

Kazuma has exactly B coins left. He wants to invite exactly 3 influencers whose total appearance fee sums to exactly B. Among all valid triplets, find the one with maximum total reach. If no valid triplet exists, print -1. Aqua will cry either way.

Note: two influencers who have collabed means their audiences overlap, so total reach is the union of all influencers reachable from any of the three via BFS, not the sum.

Input

The first line contains a single integer t (1 ≤ t ≤ 10), the number of test cases.

Each test case begins with three integers n, m, and B. The next line contains n integers c1, c2, ..., cn (-109 ≤ ci ≤ 109), the appearance fees. The next m lines each contain two integers u and v (1 ≤ u, v ≤ n, u ≠ v), a mutual collab between influencer u and influencer v.

Output

For each test case, print a single integer: the maximum total reach among all valid triplets, or -1 if no triplet sums to B.

Constraints
1 ≤ t ≤ 10
3 ≤ n ≤ 3000, 0 ≤ m ≤ n×(n-1)/2
-109 ≤ ci ≤ 109, -109 ≤ B ≤ 109
Influencers are numbered 1 to n (1-indexed). Collabs are undirected.
1 ≤ u, v ≤ n for each collab, u ≠ v. No duplicate edges.
All appearance fees are distinct.
Notes
Sample 1: valid triplets summing to 10 include influencers with fees (1,3,6). BFS from nodes 1, 2, 3 reaches all 5 influencers through the collab chain. Total reach = 5. The party was worth it. Kazuma would like credit for this. Aqua took credit for it instead.
Sample I/O
Sample 1
Input
1 5 4 10 1 3 6 2 8 1 2 2 3 3 4 4 5
Output
5
Sample 2
Input
1 4 2 100 1 2 3 4 1 2 3 4
Output
-1
